7. Focused attention. When you need help, Dad gives a 5-minute Focus Window‚Äîno phone or computer‚Äîto start/finish a task.
8. Explanations, not just orders. For non-urgent things, Dad explains the "why." For urgent/safety: follow now, explain right after.

üå± How We Learn & Grow

Section 1: Our Family Principles

1. Gentle Reminder & Redo It Right (0-10 seconds)
Quick reset - chance to do it right. Try it again the right way‚Äîkind words + the right action. If something got messy or feelings got bumped, make it right fast (fix, clean, replace, short apology, or offer 10 helper minutes).
2. Pause & Think (2-5 minutes)
Tiny pit stop. Breathe, write your Next Right Step, then do it. Timer is visible so time doesn't feel endless.
3. Break & Earn-Back
Short pause for screens/calls/shopping until a small job is finished. There's always a fast, clear way to earn it back right away.
4. Fresh Start
We pick one small change to try for a week (a tool, a swap, or a reminder). Clean slate.
