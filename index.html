üõ°Ô∏è Rules for Dad

1. Calm voice & respect
Dad uses a calm voice‚Äîno yelling, mocking, or shaming. If he's heated, he calls a 60-second Pause and resets.
2. Clear expectations
Dad shows what "done" looks like in one sentence or with a picture.
3. Chance to make it right
Before any consequence, there's a Repair Window: 5 minutes on a visible timer to fix the issue.
4. Daily 1:1 time (15 min each)
Antoine, Cassia, and Julian each get 15 minutes of distraction-free Special Time. Missed time is made up within 48 hours.
5. Opinions are heard
Dad listens fully, repeats back what he heard, considers it, and explains the decision (even when it's "no").
6. One appeal
You may make one respectful appeal per decision with new info; Dad will reconsider once, quickly.
7. Focused attention
When you need help, Dad gives a 5-minute Focus Window‚Äîno phone or computer‚Äîto start/finish a task.
8. Explanations, not just orders
For non-urgent things, Dad explains the "why." For urgent/safety: follow now, explain right after.
9. Real apology & repair
Dad uses the 3 R's‚ÄîRecognize, Repair, Restore (and models the redo).
10. Same cleanliness rules
Dad follows the same reset rules as everyone.
