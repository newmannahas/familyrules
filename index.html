🤗
1 Bodies & Space
Keep hands/feet/objects to yourself. "Stop" means stop immediately. Respect others' bubble - if someone moves away, don't follow.
💬
2 Words & Taking Turns
Calm voices (indoor volume). Wait your turn - no interrupting. Listen with your eyes and body. Show interest by asking questions!
⚡
3 First Response
Within 5 seconds say "Got it!" or ask for a quick finish time. If granted, timer on; then do it!
🏠
4 Put It Back Plus
Things live at their address. Ask before borrowing. Put it back PLUS one thing better (wipe counter, straighten cushions).
📚
5 Responsibilities First
Morning/Evening checklist AND homework before screens. When work is done, then fun!
💝
6 Honesty & Repair
Tell the truth. If you break/forget/hurt: own it → apologize → repair it.
💭
7 Think of Others First
Before asking/doing: "How will this affect others?" Share airtime - after you talk, ask about someone else!
📱
8 Tech with Care
Only in allowed places/times. Parents have passwords. No devices in bedrooms/bathrooms.
🛡️
9 Safety & Boundaries
Seatbelts, helmets, kitchen/tools with permission. Tell parents where you're going. Keep spaces guest-ready!
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
10 We're a Team
Help when you see a need. Celebrate others' wins. One kind word/act per family member daily. Family first!

🌱 How We Learn & Grow

1
Gentle Reminder: Quick reset - chance to do it right (0-10 seconds)
2
Practice Time: Let's try that again the right way!
3
Pause & Think: Take a break from the activity
  • Screens: 15 min → 30 min → rest of day
  • Items left out: Holding shelf until tomorrow
4
Make It Right: Fix what was affected (clean, replace, apology note, 10 "helper minutes")
5
Fresh Start: Quick plan together, then clean slate!

🛠️ Our Special Helper Tools

🎴
Conversation Cards
At dinner, everyone draws a card:
  • "Tell about someone else's win today"
  • "What did you notice someone needed?"
  • "Ask about their hardest part today"
💰
The Interrupting Jar
Each interruption = 25¢ or one privilege token. Money goes to the interrupted person. Watch it add up!
📊
Annoy-O-Meter
Before doing something questionable:
  1. Will this bug someone?
  2. Is it worth it?
  3. What could I do instead?
⏱️
2-Minute Rule
After 2 minutes of talking about yourself, pivot to asking about others or lose your audience!
